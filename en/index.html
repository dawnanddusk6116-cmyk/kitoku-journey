DAILY TUNING
Small choices can return you to yourself

JAPAN OS is not only about knowing your type. It is about choosing one small action that helps you feel aligned today.

Color
Choose one color that supports the state you want: clarity, calm, courage, warmth, or renewal.
Scent
Use scent lightly as a signal to your body: begin, rest, focus, soften, or let go.
Words
Keep one sentence as your compass. Not to force yourself, but to return to your center.
